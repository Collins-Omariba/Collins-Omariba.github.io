Developer Salary PredictionML Web App

CODE

This machine learning web app predicts software developer salaries based on data from the 2022 Stack Overflow survey.

It uses a Decision Tree model to predict salaries based on factors such as experience, education, programming language proficiency, and geographic location. The app also displays some graphs to provide additional insights into the data, and is an ideal tool for companies and recruiters who are looking to make data-driven hiring decisions.

The app is also useful for job seekers who want to know what to expect in terms of salary based on their experience and qualifications.

It's also user-friendly and intuitive , making it easy for anyone to use without any prior knowledge of machine learning or data analysis.
